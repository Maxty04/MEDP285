About the Art

The ongoing theme of this semester across multiple classes and projects has been nostalgia. Whether it be the "MS Paint" project or the inspiration behind sketches, the goal is to bring the viewers back to internet 2.0. The 3 pages that you will see on my website are all remnants of the past re-imagined in the artists form. Please be aware of how you may feel as you go through the pages and share anything you can at the link below.

Bio

A Korean, Brooklyn Raised, USAF vet who aspires to be one of the greats in the media world. Taking inspiration from artists such as Virgil Abloh, Kendrick Lamar, Gerald Genta, and Ralph Lauren, Max seeks to create something timeless. Something original, yet revolutionary.
